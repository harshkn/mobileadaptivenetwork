Simulation of Mobile Adaptive Network using Matlab.

Implemented as part of my seminar on advanced topics in statistical signal processing.

Simulation video

Implementation of the paper - S. Y. Tu and A. H. Sayed,”Mobile Adaptive Networks”, IEEE journal of selected topics in signal processing, vol. 5, no. 4, pp. 649-664, August 2011.

Other papers referred were -

A. H. Sayed, S. Y. Tu, J. Chen, X. Zhao, and Z. J. Towfic, ”Diffusion Strategies for Adaptation and Learning over Networks”, IEEE signal processing magazine, pp. 155-171, May 2013.

F. S. Cattivelli and A. H. Sayed, Diffusion LMS Strategies for Distributed Estimation, IEEE Transactions on Signal Processing, vol. 58, no. 3, pp. 10351048, Mar. 2010.
